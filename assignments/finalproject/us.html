About the Owner

Mike first started Mountain Spokes 10 years ago to help mountain bikers come together. With a deep love for mountain biking, he decided it was time to share it. He began by doing simple maintenance and repairs. Two years later, he received his first mountain bike inventory. After three more years of sales and repairs, Mike began sponsoring different adventure tours, including the yearly "Cycle the Continental Divide". Today Mike is an active part of the business.
