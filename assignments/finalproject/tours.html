Grotto Falls Tour

Come on an exciting bike ride to Grotto Falls!

  • Experience Level: Advanced
  • Offered: Daily
  • Route: Bozeman, MT to Mt Chisholm and back
  • Distance: 67 miles round-trip
  • Terrain: Rocky, narrow, dirt paths. Extreme uphill and downhill.
  • Cost: $115

Difficulty Levels:

  • Extreme: Only the most experienced bikers. Terrain is harsh and rocky with little or no path. Extreme curves, ups, and downs. Fast paced and intense!

  • Advanced: Involves narrow paths, fast pace, lots of ups and downs. This is a high level adventure!

  • Intermediate: Some steep portions, but time to rest between. Medium paced, with well marked dirt paths.

  • Beginner: Fairly flat routes with well marked paths. Many paths are paved. Relaxed pace. Great for families!
