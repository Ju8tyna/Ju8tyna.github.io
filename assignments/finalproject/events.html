Cycle the Continental Divide

Join us for and adventure on one of the world's longest off-pavement biking trail!

Route

  1. Helena, MT to Cotler Bay, WY (449.6 miles)
  2. Cotler Bay, WY to Rawlins, WY (365.5 miles)
  3. Rawlins, WY to Salida, CO (374.9 miles)
  4. Salida, CO to Grants, NM (539.2 miles)
  5. Grants, NM to Atelope Wells, NM (394.4 miles)

Dates

This yearly event will take place June 3rd 2019 through June 23rd

Requirements

This adventure is not for the light of heart! On this journey, 90% of the trail is either uphill or downhill. Minimum requirements include:

  • 2 years recent mountaing biking or related experience
  • At least 18 years of age (16 years of age if accompanied by guardian)
  • Bicycle inspection (inspection provided at no cost by Mountain Spoke)
  • Excellent physical condition. Physical signed by physician required.
  • Participate in 100 mile preparation ride in May (no additional cost)
  • Must bring bicycle and trailer. Bicycles and trailers available for rent upon prior request.
